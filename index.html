Hi. I'm Maxine.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on creating clean and minimalist design. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found volunteering in the vineyards, biking and trying out that new vegan recipe.

A fun fact: As a plant fanatic, I am completely enamored by anything leafy and green. I attend the Philadelphia flower show every year and would love to have the experience and opportunity to participate in the future!
